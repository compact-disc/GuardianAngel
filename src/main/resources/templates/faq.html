Frequently Asked Questions

Where and what data is stored?

Your most recent 20 Tweets are collected and stored in our database. All data relating to each Tweet is stored along with sentiment analysis data.

Is my Twitter login data kept?

No, all authentication/login is stored by Twitter.

Is my data kept after I logout?

Yes, all of the data we collect is stored in our database and kept after you logout.

What is sentiment?

A view of or attitude toward a situation or event; an opinion.

What is Stanford NLP?

Stanford NLP is Stanford Universities Natural Language Processing Group. We use their Stanford CoreNLP which is a natural language processing tool to analyze sentiment.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, you can delete all of your data from the View Tweets page.
